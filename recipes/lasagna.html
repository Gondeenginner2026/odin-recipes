Classic Meat Lasagna

Description:

This classic lasagna recipe is the ultimate comfort food, featuring layers of rich meat sauce, creamy cheeses, and tender pasta. It is a hearty Italian-American dish that brings together savory flavors of garlic, herbs, and melted mozzarella in every single bite.

Perfect for family dinners or meal prepping, this recipe is a great way to practice structuring complex information in HTML. You will learn how to organize different sections, from nested ingredient lists to the chronological order of the cooking process.

Ingredients:

  • 12 lasagna noodles (cooked);
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 jar (24 oz) marinara sauce
  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg, lightly beaten
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Prepare the Sauce: In a large pan, brown the ground beef with garlic. Stir in the marinara sauce and oregano, then simmer for 10 minutes.
  2. Make the Cheese Mix: In a small bowl, combine the ricotta cheese with the beaten egg and half of the parmesan cheese.
  3. Layer the Dish: Spread a thin layer of meat sauce in a baking dish. Place 3 noodles on top, followed by a layer of the ricotta mixture and mozzarella.
  4. Repeat: Continue layering sauce, noodles, and cheeses until all ingredients are used, finishing with a thick layer of mozzarella on top.
  5. Bake: Cover with foil and bake at 190°C (375°F) for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for another 20 minutes until the cheese is golden and bubbly.
  6. Rest: Let the lasagna rest for 15 minutes before serving so the layers stay together when sliced.
